8. Responsive Modifiers

The framework includes media query modifiers for specific breakpoints (XL, L, M, S). These are appended to existing classes:

Breakpoints: --on-xl (max 1400px), --on-l (max 992px), --on-m (max 768px), --on-s (max 480px).
Usage Example: .s-columns-2--on-m (Switches to 2 columns only on medium screens and below)
